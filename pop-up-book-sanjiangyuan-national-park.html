Pop-up book: Sanjiangyuan National Park

About the event

As the defense host for the Dachuang (College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program) project, I led the production of a children's science popularization picture book project about national parks.

The project resulted in pop-up books, a web page, related merchandise, and other deliverables. I was involved in the production and completion of every part of the project, which not only enhanced my design skills and creative abilities, but also taught me how to collaborate with team members.

Moreover, it gave me a deeper understanding of how to convey scientific knowledge to children in a simple and accessible way, sparking their interest in and concern for the protection of the natural environment.
