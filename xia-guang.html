About the event

I joined the micro film Xia Guang as a production assistant during my freshman year. My main responsibilities included on-set still photography and assisting with daily production work. Throughout the entire shooting period, I closely followed the production process and gained in-depth knowledge of film workflow, job divisions for different crew roles and team collaboration. This experience helped me fully immerse myself in professional film shooting scenarios and accumulate practical experience in video production.
