Digital Guide Design Project: Fun and Harmony at Jingshan Park

About the event

For the Jingshan Park Guide Design project, I was responsible for 3D modeling, video editing and interface design.

I collaborated closely with team members, including guide system developers and photographers. This project won the First Prize in the Beijing Division of the 11th Future Designer National University Digital Art Competition.
