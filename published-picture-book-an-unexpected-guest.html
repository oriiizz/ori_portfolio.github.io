Published Picture Book: An Unexpected Guest

About the event

This is a picture book on the theme of family affection education for children. From overall planning to creative development, and from conceptualization to the design and production of the book, it is based on the real-life love-hate relationship I have with my younger sister.

Through storytelling and illustrations, it aims to convey to children how to get along with their family members and the importance of family bonds. The picture book has now been published.
