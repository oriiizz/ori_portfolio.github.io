BJFU School of Economics and Management Website AI Redesign

About the event

Led the information architecture (IA) overhaul for the school website, performing systematic categorization and structural reorganization of multi-page content including faculty profiles, academic programs, and research information.

Conducted competitive analysis and stakeholder interviews to develop an IA strategy that balanced user needs with organizational requirements, ensuring user-centric design principles guided the restructuring.

Delivered the IA blueprint that became the foundational framework for the final developed website, demonstrating strong capabilities in managing complex systems and designing for scalability.

Details

Completed under the guidance of Professor Hu Xianming, Department of Visual Communication, Beijing Forestry University. This is a comprehensive design practice focusing on website information architecture and user experience design.
