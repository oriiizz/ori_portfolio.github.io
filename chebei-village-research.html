About the event

Research & AI Design for High-density Urban Village

This joint workshop took Chebei Village, a representative high-density urban village in Guangzhou, as the research site, focusing on its spatial revitalization potential. We carried out on-site surveys and one-on-one interviews with local residents, then organized field data and classified variables.

We further leveraged AI to create urban renewal designs, and conducted surveys to evaluate public acceptance of AI-assisted design. Combining field research, data analysis and intelligent design, this project provides practical references for the regeneration of urban villages.
