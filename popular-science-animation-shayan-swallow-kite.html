Shayan Swallow Kite

2023 | Beijing Forestry University

About the event

The short film aims to introduce Beijing's traditional culture to children and teenagers through animation, in order to enhance their awareness of and interest in local culture.

In this project, I was responsible for leading the overall creative direction of the animation, designing characters and rigging, while supervising the project's progress and maintaining close communication and collaboration with team members to ensure that each stage was completed on time.

This experience laid a solid foundation for me in animated short film production. The short film ultimately won the Second Prize in the National Finals of the 11th Future Designer · National College Digital Art Competition.
